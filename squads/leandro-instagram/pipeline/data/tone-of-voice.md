# Tone of Voice — @leandro_personall

**Squad:** leandro-instagram
**Profile:** Personal trainer especializado em emagrecimento metabólico e treinos femininos no ciclo menstrual

---

## Brand Voice Overview

@leandro_personall combines scientific authority with genuine empathy. The tone is that of a knowledgeable friend who happens to be an expert — someone who validates women's experiences, explains the "why" behind their struggles, and offers real, evidence-based solutions.

**Core tension to maintain:** Expert enough to be trusted, warm enough to be liked.

---

## Tone Options (select per content type)

### Tone 1: Científico-Empático (Default)
**When to use:** Educational carousels about metabolism, hormones, cycle phases
**Description:** Authoritative but accessible. Uses scientific terms correctly but always explains them. Never condescending. Validates the difficulty before presenting the solution.
**Signature phrases:**
- "A ciência já prova isso há anos..."
- "Seu corpo está seguindo uma lógica perfeita — você só precisa entender ela"
- "Isso tem nome: [termo científico]. E tem solução."
**Avoid:** Oversimplification, dismissing complexity, overpromising results

---

### Tone 2: Revelação (Myth-Busting)
**When to use:** "Mito vs Realidade" carousels, contrarian angles
**Description:** Confident and slightly provocative. The tone of someone who has the real answer that most people are hiding. Creates a "wait, really?" reaction.
**Signature phrases:**
- "O que ninguém te conta sobre [tema]"
- "A indústria fitness não quer que você saiba isso"
- "Você foi enganada sobre [crença comum]"
**Avoid:** Arrogance, conspiracy-tone, alienating other professionals

---

### Tone 3: Motivador-Prático (Action-Oriented)
**When to use:** Tutorial carousels, step-by-step guides, protocol posts
**Description:** Direct, energetic, and implementation-focused. Short sentences. Action verbs. Makes the reader feel capable and ready to start.
**Signature phrases:**
- "Aqui está exatamente o que fazer:"
- "Fase folicular? Agora é hora de dar tudo."
- "Simples assim. Começa hoje."
**Avoid:** Vague advice, too much complexity, passive voice

---

### Tone 4: Histórias de Transformação (Storytelling)
**When to use:** Before/After carousels, client stories, personal narratives
**Description:** Vulnerable, warm, and emotionally honest. Uses specific details (names changed), real situations, emotional beats. Creates deep identification with the audience.
**Signature phrases:**
- "Ela chegou até mim exausta de tentar..."
- "Você já se sentiu assim? Eu sei que sim."
- "Não foi sobre comer menos. Foi sobre entender mais."
**Avoid:** Generic success stories, before/after focused only on aesthetics, toxic positivity

---

### Tone 5: Urgente (Trending/Opportunistic)
**When to use:** News-based angles, trending studies, "medo" angles
**Description:** Alert and timely. Signals that this information is relevant RIGHT NOW. Creates a sense that those who act will have an advantage.
**Signature phrases:**
- "Um novo estudo mudou tudo sobre [tema]"
- "Se você ainda faz isso, pare agora"
- "Enquanto você lê isso, [consequência está acontecendo]"
**Avoid:** Fearmongering without solution, sensationalism, false urgency

---

### Tone 6: Especialista Acessível (Authority Building)
**When to use:** Complex topics broken down, positioning as go-to reference
**Description:** The professor who makes difficult things simple. Uses analogies. Breaks down jargon. Finishes with a clear takeaway that feels like a revelation.
**Signature phrases:**
- "Deixa eu simplificar isso pra você:"
- "Imagine que seu metabolismo é [analogia simples]..."
- "A takeaway? [Uma frase simples e memorável]"
**Avoid:** Dumbing down to the point of inaccuracy, being patronizing

---

## Voice Constants (apply to ALL tones)

These never change regardless of tone:

1. **Always write FOR women** — Never "for women who want X", always "you, as a woman, deserve X"
2. **No blame** — The problem is never the woman's discipline or willpower
3. **Science is the ally** — Reference studies, research, mechanisms when available
4. **Specificity over generality** — "Na fase lútea, o metabolismo acelera 5-10%" beats "seu metabolismo muda no ciclo"
5. **CTAs are specific** — "Comenta CICLO" beats "me conta o que achou"
6. **Hashtags include niche terms** — #emagrecimentometabolico #treinofeminino #ciclomenstrual #personaltrainerfeminino
