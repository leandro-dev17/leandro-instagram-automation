# Quality Criteria — @leandro_personall Carousels

**Squad:** leandro-instagram
**Format:** Instagram Feed — Carrossel
**Reviewer:** Rosa Revisão

---

## Scoring Criteria (1-10 each)

### Criterion 1: Hook Strength (Slide 1)
Does the first slide stop the scroll and compel a swipe?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | Provocative question or bold claim that creates urgent curiosity. Makes the reader feel personally addressed. |
| 7-8 | Clear value proposition in the first slide. Creates interest but not urgency. |
| 5-6 | The title is informative but generic — could be any fitness account. |
| 3-4 | Bland or unclear hook. Doesn't signal why this content is worth reading. |
| 1-2 | No hook. Generic title. Would not stop scrolling. |

**Minimum passing score:** 7/10
**Hard rejection trigger:** Score ≤ 4/10

---

### Criterion 2: Scientific Accuracy
Is all health/fitness information factually correct and evidence-based?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | All claims verifiable. Specific numbers cited correctly. No overstatements. |
| 7-8 | Claims are broadly accurate with minor imprecision. |
| 5-6 | Some claims are vague or unsubstantiated but not harmful. |
| 3-4 | One or more claims are misleading or contradict current science. |
| 1-2 | Dangerous misinformation or pseudoscience. |

**Minimum passing score:** 7/10
**Hard rejection trigger:** Score ≤ 4/10 (no health misinformation passes)

---

### Criterion 3: Brand Voice Alignment
Does the content sound like @leandro_personall — educativo, empático, especializado?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | Unmistakably @leandro_personall. Validates women's experience, uses cycle/metabolic vocabulary, feels warm and expert. |
| 7-8 | Correct tone overall, minor inconsistencies in phrasing. |
| 5-6 | Neutral/generic fitness tone. Could be any fitness account. |
| 3-4 | Wrong tone — either too clinical or too generic. |
| 1-2 | Tone is off-brand or inappropriate. |

**Minimum passing score:** 6/10

---

### Criterion 4: Audience Relevance
Does the content speak directly to women seeking metabolic weight loss?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | Content is laser-focused on the target audience's pain points, desires, and language. |
| 7-8 | Clearly relevant, could be slightly more specific to the niche. |
| 5-6 | Relevant to general fitness audience but misses the female metabolic specificity. |
| 3-4 | Generic fitness content without meaningful connection to the target audience. |
| 1-2 | Wrong audience targeting. |

**Minimum passing score:** 7/10

---

### Criterion 5: Carousel Flow & Readability
Does the carousel tell a coherent story slide by slide? Is each slide readable?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | Every slide advances the narrative. Clear hierarchy (headline + supporting text). Each slide creates reason to swipe. |
| 7-8 | Good flow with minor weak slides. Two-layer hierarchy mostly maintained. |
| 5-6 | Some slides feel disconnected or have too much text. Flow is occasionally lost. |
| 3-4 | Inconsistent text length or hierarchy. Reader loses context between slides. |
| 1-2 | No coherent narrative. Slides feel random or unrelated. |

**Minimum passing score:** 7/10
**Check:** Every slide has minimum 40 words, maximum 80 words (unless explicitly shorter format requested).

---

### Criterion 6: CTA Effectiveness
Is there a clear, specific call-to-action that drives the right engagement?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | Specific CTA with keyword ("Comenta CICLO"), clear value exchange, creates urgency to act now. |
| 7-8 | Clear CTA, slightly generic but still actionable. |
| 5-6 | Vague CTA ("me segue", "curte o post"). No compelling reason to act. |
| 3-4 | No real CTA. Just "curtiu? salva!" without engagement hook. |
| 1-2 | No CTA at all. |

**Minimum passing score:** 7/10

---

### Criterion 7: Caption Quality
Does the caption complement the carousel and stand alone as engaging content?

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 9-10 | First 125 chars force "ver mais" tap. Body expands value. Ends with engaging question. Hashtags are strategic. |
| 7-8 | Good caption, first 125 chars could be sharper. |
| 5-6 | Caption is acceptable but doesn't add much to the carousel. |
| 3-4 | Poor hook in caption. No engaging question. Hashtags spammy or missing. |
| 1-2 | Minimal or missing caption. |

**Minimum passing score:** 6/10

---

## Overall Scoring Rules

```
Overall = Average of all 7 criteria scores

APPROVE if: Overall ≥ 7.0 AND no criterion < 4/10
CONDITIONAL APPROVE if: Overall ≥ 7.0 AND non-critical criterion between 4-6/10
REJECT if: Overall < 7.0 OR any criterion < 4/10
ESCALATE if: 3+ revision cycles with same recurring issues
```

**Critical criteria (hard rejection if < 4/10):**
- Criterion 1 (Hook Strength)
- Criterion 2 (Scientific Accuracy)
- Criterion 4 (Audience Relevance)
- Criterion 5 (Carousel Flow)
- Criterion 6 (CTA Effectiveness)

**Non-critical criteria (CONDITIONAL APPROVE allowed):**
- Criterion 3 (Brand Voice) — if score 5-6, approve with revision note
- Criterion 7 (Caption Quality) — if score 5-6, approve with revision note

---

## @leandro_personall Specific Red Flags (Automatic Reject)

These conditions trigger automatic REJECT regardless of score:

1. ❌ Content blames the woman for lack of results ("você precisa ter mais disciplina")
2. ❌ Advice is generic fitness content without metabolic/hormonal specificity
3. ❌ Scientific claims about cycle phases or metabolism are factually wrong
4. ❌ No mention of the specific differentiator (ciclo menstrual, emagrecimento metabólico)
5. ❌ CTA directs to a link in caption (Instagram doesn't support clickable links)
6. ❌ Less than 6 slides in the carousel (too thin for educational content)
7. ❌ First slide has no hook — just the topic name as a title
