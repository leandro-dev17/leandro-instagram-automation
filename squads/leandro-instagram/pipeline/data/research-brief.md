## Domain 1: Instagram Carousel Content for Fitness/Health

### Frameworks & Methodologies

**The Edu-Entertainment Framework**
Fitness content that performs on Instagram blends educational value with entertainment. Pure scientific content without emotional appeal underperforms. Pure entertainment without substance creates no authority. The sweet spot: teach something specific and make it feel relevant to the reader's daily life.

**The AIDA Carousel Structure**
- **Attention** (Slide 1): Bold visual hook + provocative claim or question
- **Interest** (Slides 2-3): Context that makes the problem personal
- **Desire** (Slides 4-7): The solution, framework, or insight
- **Action** (Slide 8-10): CTA with clear next step

**Content Pillars for Fitness Educators**
Top-performing fitness educators structure their content around 4-5 pillars:
1. Education (how/why something works scientifically)
2. Myth-busting (challenging common misconceptions)
3. Practical application (what to do, step by step)
4. Transformation proof (results, before/after)
5. Motivation/mindset (emotional resonance, persistence)

### Output Examples Found in Research

**High-performing carousel types for fitness:**
- "Os 3 erros que impedem seu emagrecimento" — Problema-Solução format
- "Por que você não emagrece mesmo fazendo dieta" — Mito vs Realidade
- "O que ninguém te fala sobre treino no período menstrual" — Editorial/Tese
- "5 sinais de que seu metabolismo está lento" — Listicle

### Common Mistakes
1. Too much text per slide (reader gives up)
2. No clear differentiation between slides (feels like a wall of text)
3. Generic stock photos (signals low effort)
4. Posting without engaging with comments in first 60 minutes
5. Copy-pasting the same hashtag block every post

### Quality Benchmarks
- Saves rate: 3-8% of reach (for educational content)
- Share rate: 1-3% of reach
- Comment rate: 0.5-2% of reach
- First slide swipe-through: >40% (benchmark for carousel engagement)

## Domain 2: Personal Training Copywriting for Women — Metabolic Weight Loss

### Key Psychological Triggers for this Audience

**Primary fears:**
- Doing everything right and still not seeing results
- Being told to eat less and exercise more (been there, done that)
- Hormonal issues making weight loss impossible
- Losing muscle while losing fat
- The scale not moving despite effort

**Primary desires:**
- Understanding their own body
- Feeling in control of their metabolism
- Sustainable results (not yo-yo dieting)
- Feeling energetic, not depleted
- Understanding the female cycle connection to fitness

**Awareness level for @leandro_personall's audience:**
Mostly Problem Aware to Solution Aware — they know they struggle to lose weight, many have tried multiple approaches, and they're looking for something that actually explains WHY standard advice isn't working for them.

### Effective Copywriting Angles for Female Fitness
1. **Scientific authority** — Reference hormones, metabolism, cycle phases by name
2. **The insider knowledge** — "O que a ciência já sabe mas os personal trainers convencionais ainda não ensinaram"
3. **Validation** — Acknowledging that it's NOT the woman's fault (body works differently)
4. **Specificity** — Concrete protocols tied to cycle phase (follicular, ovulatory, luteal, menstrual)
5. **Transformation** — Emotional stories of clients who broke through plateaus

### Language Patterns That Work
**Power phrases:**
- "Seu corpo não está errado — o treino está"
- "A ciência do ciclo menstrual mudou como eu treino minhas alunas"
- "Emagrecimento metabólico ≠ cortar calorias"
- "Fase lútea" / "Fase folicular" (shows expertise, educates)
- "Resistência à insulina" (speaks to root causes)
- "Déficit calórico inteligente" (not just "coma menos")

**Avoid:**
- "Seja disciplinada" (blames the woman)
- "Faça mais cardio" (generic, discredited)
- "Só depende de você" (ignores hormonal reality)
- "Dieta" as a concept (implies temporary restriction)

## Domain 3: Female Cycle Training Content Frameworks

### The 4-Phase Training Framework (key knowledge for Ivan Instagram agent)

**Fase Menstrual (dias 1-5):**
- Estrogênio e progesterona baixos
- Ideal: treinos de baixa intensidade, yoga, caminhadas
- Conteúdo: empático, foca em recovery e autocuidado

**Fase Folicular (dias 6-13):**
- Estrogênio em ascensão
- Ideal: treinos de força, HIIT, alta intensidade
- Conteúdo: motivacional, "esta é sua semana de força"

**Fase Ovulatória (dias 14-17):**
- Pico de estrogênio e testosterona
- Ideal: treinos de máxima intensidade, recordes pessoais
- Conteúdo: desafiador, "empurre os seus limites agora"

**Fase Lútea (dias 18-28):**
- Progesterona domina
- Ideal: treinos moderados, pilates, foco em resistência
- Conteúdo: educativo sobre TPM, nutrição anti-inflamatória

### Content Angles Specific to Cycle Training
- "Treinar contra o seu ciclo está sabotando seus resultados"
- "Por que você se sente fraca na academia toda mês (e o que fazer)"
- "A semana certa para bater seu recorde na academia"
- "Como adaptar sua alimentação para cada fase do ciclo"

## Domain 4: Metabolic Weight Loss Content Framework

### Key Concepts for Educational Content

**Metabolic adaptation** — When the body downregulates metabolism in response to caloric restriction. This is why "eat less" alone fails.

**NEAT (Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis)** — Daily movement outside exercise. Content angle: why fidgeting, walking, and daily activity matter more than most think.

**Hormonal factors affecting female metabolism:**
- Insulin resistance (very relevant for PCOS audience)
- Thyroid function
- Cortisol and stress response
- Leptin and hunger hormones
- Cycle-linked metabolic changes (BMR increases by 5-10% in luteal phase)

### High-Value Content Frameworks for Metabolism
1. "Os 5 motivos pelos quais você não emagrece" (listicle)
2. "Como o estresse está engordando você" (mechanism explanation)
3. "Metabolismo lento: mito ou realidade?" (myth-busting)
4. "O que acontece no seu corpo quando você faz dieta por muito tempo" (educational)
5. "Por que seu corpo resiste ao emagrecimento (e como reverter)" (problem-solution)
