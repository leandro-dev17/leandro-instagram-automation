# Domain Framework — Fábrica de Carrosséis @leandro_personall

**Squad:** leandro-instagram
**Domain:** Instagram carousel content creation for female metabolic weight loss & cycle training

---

## Operational Framework

This framework governs how the squad approaches content creation for @leandro_personall. Every squad run follows this sequence:

### Step 1: Research Phase
**Goal:** Find the most relevant and timely news/trend in female health, fitness, or metabolic weight loss.

**Inputs:** Research focus (topic + time range from checkpoint)
**Outputs:** Ranked list of 3-5 stories/topics with relevance scores

**Criteria for story selection:**
- Relevance to target audience (women seeking metabolic weight loss)
- Timeliness (recent study, trending topic, seasonal content)
- Angle potential (story can support 3+ distinct emotional angles)
- Expertise window (topic where @leandro_personall's specialization adds genuine value)
- Engagement signal (topic already showing high engagement in the fitness creator space)

---

### Step 2: Angle Generation Phase
**Goal:** From ONE selected story, generate 5 distinct emotional angles.

**Definition of an angle:**
An angle is NOT a different topic — it is the same topic viewed through a different emotional lens or narrative perspective.

**The 5 Mandatory Angle Types:**
1. 🔴 **Medo/Urgência** — "Em X meses, mulheres que não fizerem isso vão [consequência]"
2. 🟢 **Oportunidade** — "Esta descoberta muda tudo para quem quer [resultado]"
3. 📚 **Educacional** — "O que a ciência diz sobre [tema] que sua personal ainda não te contou"
4. ↔️ **Contrário/Provocativo** — "[Crença popular] — o que ninguém te conta"
5. ⭐ **Inspiracional** — "[Transformação possível] quando você entende [mecanismo]"

**Quality bar for angles:**
- Each angle must generate a different emotional response in the reader
- The first slide hook must be 100% different between angles (not just phrasing variants)
- The CTA can vary between angles (save vs. comment vs. share)

---

### Step 3: Content Creation Phase
**Goal:** Transform the selected angle into a complete carousel (copy + caption + hashtags).

**Carousel structure for @leandro_personall (Econômico mode):**
- 8 slides (optimal for algorithm and reader attention)
- Format chosen per content goal (see formats below)
- Each slide: headline (bold, large) + supporting text (scientific/practical detail)
- Visual direction per slide: colors, photo suggestions, accent keywords

**Recommended formats for @leandro_personall:**
| Angle Type | Best Format |
|-----------|-------------|
| Medo/Urgência | Problema → Solução |
| Oportunidade | Editorial/Tese |
| Educacional | Tutorial/Passo-a-passo OR Listicle |
| Contrário | Mito vs Realidade |
| Inspiracional | Storytelling OR Antes e Depois |

**Caption requirements:**
- Hook (first 125 chars): Must force the "ver mais" tap
- Body: 3-5 key points from the carousel, expanded
- Question: End with open-ended question for comments
- CTA: Specific action (e.g., "Comenta CICLO que te mando o protocolo")
- Hashtags: 8-12 hashtags (mix: niche + mid-range + broad)

---

### Step 4: Visual Design Phase
**Goal:** Create 8 HTML/CSS slides rendered as 1080x1440px PNG images.

**Design System for @leandro_personall:**
- **Primary color:** Deep navy (#1A1F36) — authority, trust
- **Secondary color:** Warm white (#F8F6F1) — clean, approachable
- **Accent color:** Coral/terracotta (#E8614A) — energy, femininity
- **Font family:** Inter (web-safe via Google Fonts @import)
- **Slide dimensions:** 1080 × 1440px (3:4 portrait)
- **Brand elements:** @leandro_personall handle on every slide (top-right corner)

**Visual rhythm rule:** Alternate dark/light/dark backgrounds across slides.

**Typography scale (Instagram Carousel):**
- Hero text: 72px, weight 700
- Headline: 58px, weight 700
- Supporting text: 38px, weight 500
- Caption/label: 28px, weight 500
- Handle watermark: 26px, weight 400

---

### Step 5: Review Phase
**Goal:** Ensure content meets @leandro_personall's brand standards and Instagram best practices.

**Review dimensions:**
1. **Scientific accuracy** — No unverified health claims
2. **Brand voice** — Educativo + Inspirador, not generic fitness
3. **Audience relevance** — Resonates specifically with women seeking metabolic weight loss
4. **Platform optimization** — Follows instagram-feed.md best practices
5. **CTA effectiveness** — Has a specific, actionable ask

**Verdict thresholds:**
- APPROVE: All criteria ≥ 7/10, no criterion < 4/10
- CONDITIONAL APPROVE: Overall ≥ 7/10 with minor fixes noted
- REJECT: Any criterion < 4/10 or overall < 7/10

---

### Step 6: Publishing Phase
**Goal:** Publish the approved carousel to @leandro_personall's Instagram.

**Publishing requirements:**
- 2-10 images (JPEG format preferred)
- Caption with hashtags
- Confirm INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN and INSTAGRAM_USER_ID are set
- Use instagram-publisher skill
- Log the published post URL to output/
